Merge

Merge records quickly with the merge feature. You may merge up to 5 records at a time. For your convenience, only data which is different between records is shown â€” you may choose to see all data by pressing "More".

Select the desired information to keep from each record by using the radio buttons. If you don't see the correct information you mat edit the Primary record.

At any time during the merge process you mat choose to Preview the final record in the right-hand side panel.

Once you have completed your merge selections, press Save and you will be left with 1 record, the others will be archived and removed from all list views. All related records will be linked to the new merged record.

Quick tip:
It is best to use this feature in the Expanded view to see all merging records.
