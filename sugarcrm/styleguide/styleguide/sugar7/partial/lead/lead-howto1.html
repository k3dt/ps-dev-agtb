How to create a lead

Please fill in all the required fields. A required field is any field that does not have a placeholder of optional.

When finished you can chose to save and be returned to the opportunity list view, save and view which will bring you to a record view or save and create another record.

The primary fields for a record are shown. If additional fields are provided, you can click on the more link at the bottom of the primary fields to expose more detail to provide.
