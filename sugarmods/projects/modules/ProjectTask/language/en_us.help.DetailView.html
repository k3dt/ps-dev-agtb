Project Tasks

Use this page to view and edit the details of a project task.

  • To edit the details, click Edit, make the necessary changes and click Save.
  • To edit the task in grid view, click Edit Task in Grid.
  • To schedule a meeting, a call, or compose email, click the appropriate button in the Activities sub-panel.

The History sub-panel lists existing notes and files attached to the project task.

  • To attach a note or a file, click Create Note or Attachment.
  • To create an archived email, click Archive Email, copy the email contents in the Body field and save it.
  • To view a summary of the history, click View Summary.
