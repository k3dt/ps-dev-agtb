Project Template Detail Page

Use this page to view the details of a project template.

  • To edit the template, click Edit, make the necessary revisions, and click Save.
  • To duplicate the template, click Duplicate. The system displays the new template in the list on the Project Templates Home page. You can then make modifications and save it as a different template.
  • To delete the template, click Delete.
  • To view a Gantt chart of the template, click View Gantt.
  • To create a new project from the template, click Save as Project.

You can view related projects tasks in the sub-panel below.

  • To view a Gantt charts of the projects, click View Gantt.
  • To view the details of a task, click its name in the tasks list.
